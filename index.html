Entrepreneurship

As Co-founder and Chief Information Officer at Wematics, I am committed to advancing the use of sky cameras for solar applications by developing an AI-driven next-generation sky camera. This technology offers real-time cloud tracking and solar irradiance forecasting, empowering grid operators and energy providers to optimize renewable energy integration and reduce costs.
