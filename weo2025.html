Abstract

The IEA's flagship World Energy Outlook (WEO) is the most authoritative source of global energy analysis and projections. Updated annually to reflect the latest energy data, technology and market trends, and government policies, it explores a range of possible energy futures and their implications for energy security, access and emissions. The WEO-2025 uses three main scenarios — the Current Policies Scenario (CPS), the Stated Policies Scenario (STEPS), and the Net Zero Emissions by 2050 (NZE) Scenario — to illuminate the central choices, consequences and contingencies that lie ahead. A new Accelerating Clean Cooking and Electricity Services (ACCESS) scenario provides a roadmap to universal energy access. The 2025 edition comes against a backdrop of major shifts in energy policies, acute geopolitical strains, and the transformative rise of artificial intelligence — ushering in a new Age of Electricity.
